Goddess Saraswati

Goddess Saraswati is the Hindu goddess of knowledge, music, art, wisdom, and learning. She is often depicted dressed in white, seated on a lotus, and playing the veena, symbolizing purity and creative energy. Saraswati is revered by students and scholars, and her blessings are sought for success in education and the arts. The festival of Vasant Panchami is dedicated to her, celebrating the power of wisdom and the pursuit of knowledge.

Lord Ganesha

Lord Ganesha, also known as Ganapati, is one of the most revered deities in Hinduism. Recognized by his elephant head, he is the remover of obstacles, patron of arts and sciences, and the god of intellect and wisdom. Ganesha is worshipped at the beginning of rituals and ceremonies and is celebrated during the festival of Ganesh Chaturthi. His symbolism includes wisdom, understanding, and a discriminating intellect that one must possess to attain perfection in life.

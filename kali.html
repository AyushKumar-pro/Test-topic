Goddess Kali

Goddess Kali is a powerful and revered deity in Hinduism, known as the goddess of time, change, destruction, and empowerment. She is often depicted with a fierce expression, dark skin, and a necklace of skulls, symbolizing her role in destroying evil and protecting the innocent. Kali represents the transformative power of time and the cycles of creation and destruction. She is worshipped for her ability to remove negative energies and grant liberation, and is especially honored during the festival of Kali Puja.
